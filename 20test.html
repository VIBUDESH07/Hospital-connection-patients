Medical Tests Information

Blood Test

Before the Test:

  • Fast for 8-12 hours before the test.
  • Avoid heavy exercise and stressful activities prior to the test.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal diet and activities.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

Urine Test

Before the Test:

  • Collect a clean urine sample in a sterile container.
  • Follow any specific instructions provided by your doctor.

After the Test:

  • Dispose of the urine sample properly.
  • Wash your hands thoroughly.

MRI Scan (Magnetic Resonance Imaging)

Before the Test:

  • Remove any metal objects and inform the technician if you have any metal implants.
  • Wear comfortable clothing without any metal zippers or buttons.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal activities.
  • Follow up with your doctor for the results.

X-Ray Test (Radiography)

Before the Test:

  • Remove any metal objects or jewelry.
  • Wear a hospital gown if required.

After the Test:

  • Follow your doctor's instructions for any further steps.
  • Wait for the results to be analyzed.

CT Scan (Computed Tomography)

Before the Test:

  • Fast for 4-6 hours before the test, especially if contrast dye will be used.
  • Inform your doctor if you have any allergies, kidney problems, or diabetes.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal diet and activities.
  • Drink plenty of fluids to help flush out the contrast dye.

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

Before the Test:

  • Wear loose clothing that allows access to your chest.
  • Avoid oily or greasy lotions on your chest before the test.

After the Test:

  • No special precautions are usually needed after an ECG.
  • Discuss the results with your doctor during your follow-up appointment.

EEG (Electroencephalogram)

Before the Test:

  • Wash your hair before the test, but do not use any hair products like gels or sprays.
  • Follow any specific instructions provided by your doctor, such as avoiding caffeine.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal activities.
  • Discuss the results with your doctor during your follow-up appointment.

Endoscopy

Before the Test:

  • Fasting for 6-8 hours before the test, depending on the type of endoscopy.
  • Inform your doctor about any medications you are taking and any health conditions you have.

After the Test:

  • Rest until the effects of the sedation wear off.
  • Follow any dietary restrictions provided by your doctor.

Colonoscopy

Before the Test:

  • Cleanse your colon as instructed by your doctor.
  • Follow any dietary restrictions provided by your doctor.

After the Test:

  • Drink plenty of fluids to prevent dehydration.
  • Resume your normal diet unless instructed otherwise by your doctor.

Sugar Test

Before the Test:

  • Fast overnight for 8-12 hours before the test.
  • Avoid consuming sugary foods or beverages before the test.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal diet unless instructed otherwise by your doctor.
  • Monitor your blood sugar levels as advised by your healthcare provider.

Ultrasound

Before the Test:

  • Follow any fasting instructions provided by your doctor, if necessary.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and avoid wearing jewelry around the area being examined.

After the Test:

  • No special precautions are usually needed after an ultrasound.
  • Discuss the results with your doctor during your follow-up appointment.

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Before the Test:

  • Avoid caffeine, alcohol, and smoking for at least 30 minutes before the test.
  • Sit quietly for 5 minutes before the test begins.

After the Test:

  • No special precautions are usually needed after a blood pressure monitoring session.
  • Follow any recommendations provided by your doctor regarding blood pressure management.

Stomach Test (Ulcer Diagnosis)

Before the Test:

  • Fast for 6-8 hours before the test, depending on the specific instructions provided by your doctor.
  • Inform your doctor about any medications you are taking and any health conditions you have.

After the Test:

  • Follow any post-procedure instructions provided by your doctor, which may include dietary restrictions.
  • Monitor for any unusual symptoms and report them to your healthcare provider.

Spirometry (Lung Function Test)

Before the Test:

  • Avoid smoking and eating a heavy meal for at least 1 hour before the test.
  • Wear loose clothing that does not restrict your chest movement.

After the Test:

  • No special precautions are usually needed after a spirometry test.
  • Discuss the results with your doctor during your follow-up appointment.

Stress Test (Exercise Electrocardiogram)

Before the Test:

  • Wear comfortable clothing and walking shoes suitable for exercise.
  • Avoid heavy meals, caffeine, and smoking for at least 3 hours before the test.

After the Test:

  • Rest for a while after the test, as you may feel tired or lightheaded.
  • Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated.

Allergy Test

Before the Test:

  • Stop taking antihistamines several days before the test, if possible.
  • Discuss any concerns or medications with your doctor before the test.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal activities unless advised otherwise by your doctor.
  • Follow any recommendations provided by your doctor based on the test results.

Genetic Testing

Before the Test:

  • Discuss the implications and potential outcomes of genetic testing with a genetic counselor.
  • Understand the consent process and any associated costs with the test.

After the Test:

  • Wait for the results to be analyzed and interpreted by a geneticist or healthcare provider.
  • Discuss the results and any follow-up steps with your healthcare provider.

Liver Function Test

Before the Test:

  • Fast for 8-12 hours before the test, if required by your doctor.
  • Avoid alcohol and certain medications as instructed by your doctor.

After the Test:

  • Resume your normal diet and activities unless instructed otherwise by your doctor.
  • Discuss the results and any necessary follow-up with your doctor.

Biopsy

Before the Test:

  • Discuss any concerns with your doctor and ask about any specific instructions for your biopsy.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and plan for some rest after the procedure.

After the Test:

  • Rest at home for the remainder of the day.
  • Follow any post-biopsy care instructions provided by your doctor.

Mammogram

Before the Test:

  • Do not apply deodorant, talcum powder, or lotion under your arms or on your breasts on the day of the test.
  • Schedule the test for the week after your menstrual period, when your breasts are less tender.

After the Test:

  • No special precautions are usually needed after a mammogram.
  • Follow any recommendations provided by your doctor regarding regular screening.
